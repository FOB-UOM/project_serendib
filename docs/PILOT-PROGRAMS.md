# School & Faculty Pilot Programs (P2)

## Objective
Run practical pilots that validate the teacher and sustainability data loops with real users before broad scaling.

## Pilot Tracks

### Track A: School Classroom Pilot
- 2 schools, 2 teachers per school, 4-week pilot
- Focus: teacher-empowerment + sustainability-climate tasks
- KPI: number of approved contributions and teacher usefulness score

### Track B: Faculty Student Curator Pilot
- 10 student curators in rotating weekly moderation shifts
- Focus: public submission moderation and quality scoring
- KPI: moderation turnaround time and acceptance/rejection consistency

### Track C: OCR Improvement Pilot
- Community OCR correction tasks through Public Task Studio
- Focus: uncertain OCR outputs routed to review queue
- KPI: reduction in uncertain OCR share over time

## Deliverables per pilot cycle
1. Pilot plan and participant list
2. Weekly metrics snapshot
3. Pilot retrospective and improvement actions
